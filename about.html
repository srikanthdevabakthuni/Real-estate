Who We Are

Built on Trust, Driven by Excellence

PropEstate was founded in 2015 with a single mission — to make real estate in Hyderabad simple, transparent, and accessible for everyone. What started as a small team of 5 property consultants in Banjara Hills has grown into a full-service real estate company with over 150 RERA-certified agents operating across all major localities.

We believe every family deserves to find the right home without stress or confusion. That's why we go beyond just listings — we offer end-to-end support including legal verification, home loans, GHMC approvals, and post-sale assistance, all under one roof.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Every decision we make is guided by these principles.

Transparency First

We believe in complete openness — from property documents to agent fees. No hidden costs, no surprises. What you see is exactly what you get.

Client-Centric Approach

Your needs come first. Our agents take time to understand your budget, lifestyle, and future goals before suggesting any property.

RERA Compliance

Every agent and listing on our platform is RERA-verified. We hold ourselves to the highest legal standards in the industry.

Local Expertise

Our agents live and breathe Hyderabad. From Jubilee Hills to Miyapur, we have deep knowledge of every locality's micro-market.

Community Focused

We're not just selling properties — we're building communities. We work with builders who prioritise quality, green spaces, and connectivity.

Technology Driven

From virtual tours to digital documentation, we leverage the latest technology to make your property journey faster and smarter.

How We Grew

Our Journey So Far

From a modest office in Banjara Hills to one of Hyderabad's most recognised real estate brands — here are the milestones that shaped us.

2015 — Founded
PropEstate Opens Its Doors

Started with 5 agents in a small Banjara Hills office. Closed our first 10 deals in the first month, mostly residential apartments in HITEC City.

2017 — Expansion
Expanded to 8 Hyderabad Localities

Opened dedicated desks for Jubilee Hills, Gachibowli, Kondapur and Madhapur. Team grew to 30 agents. Crossed 1,000 successful transactions.

2019 — Digital Launch
Launched PropEstate Online Platform

Introduced our digital property listings portal with virtual tours, online documentation, and real-time market data. Monthly traffic reached 50,000 users.

2021 — RERA Certified
Became Fully TSRERA Compliant

All 80+ agents achieved full RERA certification under TSRERA. Became one of the first Hyderabad firms to mandate RERA compliance across the entire team.

2023 — Recognition
Best Real Estate Company — Hyderabad

Awarded "Best Real Estate Company in Hyderabad" at the Telangana Real Estate Awards. Crossed ₹500 Crore in total transactions and 10,000 families served.

2025 — Today
150 Agents, 5,200+ Active Listings

Now Hyderabad's most trusted full-service real estate platform — covering sales, rentals, home loans, legal services and property management.
